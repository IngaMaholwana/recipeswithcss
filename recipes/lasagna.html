Lasagna

Description

A hearty cheesey deliciousness perfect for those evening family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole onion.
  • Noodles
  • Mince meat
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Gouda Cheese
  • Spices and other flavours of choice

preparation instructions

  1. pre heat the oven at 180* celsius
  2. Bake for about 20-30 minutes
  3. serve up for 4
